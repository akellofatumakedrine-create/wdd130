Uganda

Uganda, known as the "Pearl of Africa," is a landlocked country in East Africa renowned for its diverse wildlife, including mountain gorillas in Bwindi Impenetrable Forest. With stunning landscapes from Lake Victoria to the Rwenzori Mountains, Uganda offers rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality.
