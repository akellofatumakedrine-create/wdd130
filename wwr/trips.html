Featured Trips

Half Day Adventure

Perfect for families and first-timers! Experience the thrill of gentle to moderate rapids in a scenic canyon setting. Our guides ensure a safe and memorable experience for everyone.

⏱ Duration: 4 hours

Big Mallard Challenge

For intermediate to advanced paddlers! Navigate through some of the most exciting rapids in the region. This trip combines adrenaline-pumping action with stunning river views.

⏱ Duration: Full Day (7 hours)

Los Dientes del Diablo

For expert rafters only! Conquer the legendary "Devil's Teeth" rapids - the most challenging and rewarding experience Wild Wave has to offer. Not for the faint of heart!

⏱ Duration: Full Day + (8+ hours)

Sunset Float

Unwind with a peaceful evening on the water. This relaxing float is perfect for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty. Watch the sun paint the sky as you drift along serene waters.

⏱ Duration: 3 hours

Extreme Challenge

The ultimate test of your rafting skills! Combine multiple intense rapids with technical maneuvers. This is for serious adventurers ready to push their limits and create unforgettable memories.

⏱ Duration: Full Day+ (9+ hours)
