History

Founded in 2005 by outdoor enthusiasts with a passion for river adventures, White Water Rafting Adventures began as a small operation with just two rafts and a dream. Over the years, we've grown into a premier rafting destination, serving thousands of adventurers annually. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and customer satisfaction has earned us recognition as one of the top rafting companies in the region. We continue to expand our offerings while maintaining the personal touch and attention to detail that set us apart from the beginning.
